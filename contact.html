What We Can Help With

Found an error in one of our articles or a pay rate that needs updating? Let us know — accuracy is our top priority and we appreciate corrections from veterans who know the system.

Is there a condition, benefit, or claims situation we haven't covered yet? Suggest it and we'll add it to our content roadmap — veteran input directly shapes what we write.

⚠️ What We Cannot Help With

We are not a VA-accredited claims agent, attorney, or VSO. We cannot review your personal claim, provide legal advice, tell you what rating you should receive, or represent you before the VA. For those services — which are available completely free — please contact a VSO or VA-accredited representative.

Free Claims Help — Contact These Instead

For personalized VA disability claims help, these organizations are accredited, free, and can actually represent you before the VA — something we cannot do:

Free VA Claims Assistance Resources

  • DAV (Disabled American Veterans) — dav.org · 1-800-827-1000 · Largest veterans VSO in the US
  • VFW (Veterans of Foreign Wars) — vfw.org · Free service officers nationwide
  • American Legion — legion.org · Local chapters in every state
  • VA Direct — va.gov · 1-800-827-1000 · Official VA claims line
  • Your State VA Office — Search "[your state] department of veterans affairs" for local accredited help
